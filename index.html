Guest Arrival
Check In
Follow these steps in order

Process

1
Let them in → bring to reception → find name on computer
2
Ask for passport (Australians can use Australian ID) — no ID = no check-in
3
Ask them to fill out the form on their phone
4
Refresh until form is submitted
5
Check passport matches the photo on the form
6
Paid online → return passport | Not paid → keep passport at reception until morning
7
Offer towel rental $2 + $5 deposit | Padlocks $5
8
Take key $20 deposit payment
9
Print merchent reciept and write name down
10
Give room key + lanyard
11
Tell them about the hostel, events & group chat
12
Tell them where their room is (see Rooms tab)
